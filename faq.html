Frequently Asked Questions

San José Works is a job assistance program for youth residents of San Jose who are 16 to 29 years old.

San José works provides employment and career services including job assistance, resume building, job fairs, and supportive services.

If you are between the ages of 16-29 years of age and are a resident of San Jose, you qualify.

No cost, this is a free service to our youth job seekers.

San José Works provides paid work experience opportunities to students ages 14-18 years of age attending high school.
