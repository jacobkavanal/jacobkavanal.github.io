ABOUT US

San José Works (SJ Works) is a partnership with work2future and the Mayor's Gang Prevention Task Force. Operated by work2future, San José Works began in 2015-2016 and so far has served over 5,000 youth. This program provides youth with employment services and critical life skill instruction, including leadership development, financial literacy, job counseling, mentoring, job readiness training, and supportive services such as transportation and clothing. Each year San José Works places youth ages 14 to 18 tracts into the Paid Work Experience Program for subsidized employment opportunities and youth ages 16 to 29 with barriers to employment into the Employment and Career Services Program for opportunities with private employers.

The strategic focus for San José Works is to connect youth to career pathways in high growth sectors, such as engineering, information technology, finance, advanced manufacturing, health care, and construction. San José Works partners with the Manufacturing, Computer Technology, Healthcare, and other in-demand industries to promote the private sector, non-profit, and government careers. San José Works creates meaningful employment opportunities to help underserved youth build towards college and careers.
